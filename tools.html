📜 Incoterms – International Trade Rules

Incoterms (International Commercial Terms) define the obligations, risks, and costs between buyers and sellers in global trade.

These standardized terms are essential for businesses involved in importing and exporting goods worldwide.

🔹 Common Incoterms: EXW, FOB, CIF, DDP, etc.

🔹 Purpose: Avoid misunderstandings in international shipments.

🔹 Who Uses It? Freight forwarders, importers, and exporters.

Understanding Incoterms reduces disputes, ensures smooth customs clearance, and optimizes shipping costs.

📏 Metric Conversion – Accurate Unit Conversions

Logistics requires precise weight, volume, and dimension calculations to ensure smooth operations.

The metric conversion tool helps you switch between kilograms, pounds, cubic meters, gallons, feet, and more.

🔹 Weight: kg ↔ lbs (Pounds)

🔹 Volume: Liters ↔ Gallons

🔹 Distance: Meters ↔ Feet

A wrong unit conversion can lead to excess freight charges, incorrect packaging, or shipment rejection.

This tool ensures accuracy and compliance with global shipping standards.

📦 Container Dimensions – Know Your Space

Shipping containers come in various sizes and capacities, and selecting the right one is crucial for cost-effective transportation.

This tool provides details on standard and high cube containers, weight limits, and cargo capacity.

🔹 Standard Sizes: 20ft, 40ft, 45ft containers

🔹 Capacity: How much cargo each container can hold

🔹 Usage: Helps in choosing the best container for your goods

Understanding container dimensions ensures you avoid extra charges, optimize space, and prevent overloading penalties.
